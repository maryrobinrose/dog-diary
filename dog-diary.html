My Life as a Dog

I am an interesting dog with an interesting life

October 1st, 2018

I had a very exciting morning. I found and chased four different squirrels on our walk to the park. I smelled all of my familiar friends plus a poodle I didn't know named Daisy.

October 2nd, 2018

I got sick today! Yuck! Mom was very grumpy with me, but she cleaned up the mess anyway! She is a good human. I like my people, they are very nice to me. Gotta go chase a cat, can't talk longer.

October 3rd, 2018

I was contemplating the meaning of life this morning. I was stuck deciding whether I do in fact have free will, when I smelled some food and completely lost my train of thought.

October 4th, 2018

I miss my other humans. They have been gone for a long time and rarely come around anymore. Did I do something to make them... RABBIT! MUST CHASE! GET THE RABBIT!
